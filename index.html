3. Regulatory Context & Competitive Landscape

Thailand's medical device framework aligns with IMDRF and ASEAN Medical Device Directive. Thai FDA offers Concise Pathway, Reliance Program, and Full Evaluation, accepting approvals from reference countries (US FDA, EU Notified Bodies, etc.). This creates commercial advantage for established international manufacturers.

Market Structure: Import-driven IVD sector. In recent years, the top supplying regions include [major western economies] and [Asian suppliers] with varying market shares.

Competitive Landscape Overview: The IVD market is consolidated among global leaders. Western manufacturers hold dominant positions due to regulatory synergy. Chinese manufacturers have moderate presence, mostly in mid-tier segments.

Opportunity area: clinical chemistry pre-mixes (glucose, LDH, creatinine, ALT, AST), modular reagent systems, and raw material supply to local manufacturers/distributors.

4. Thai FDA Registration Pathways for IVD Products

For raw materials, reagents and pre-mixes, the following registration pathways are available via local importer/distributor:

PathwayDescriptionTimelineKey Suitability
Concise PathwayFor products with prior approval from reference countries (US FDA, EU CE, etc.)~90-180 daysStrong if manufacturer holds EU CE or reference approval
Full Evaluation PathwayWithout reference country approval; complete technical file required~180-365 daysPossible but longer timeline
Reliance ProgramFor products registered in ASEAN reference countries~60-120 daysGood for ASEAN presence
OEM/Private Label RegistrationLocal manufacturer/importer registers product under its name~90-180 daysRecommended for market entry — distributor holds license
Strategic recommendation: Partner with a local Thai distributor who will hold Thai FDA registration via OEM/Private Label pathway, minimizing regulatory burden while ensuring compliance.
